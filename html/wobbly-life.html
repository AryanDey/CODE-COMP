About the Game

Wobbly Life is a sandbox physics game where you explore, complete tasks, and have fun in a wobbly world.

How to Install

Wobbly Life is available on PC (Steam), Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo Switch. On PC, visit the Steam page to purchase and install the game. For Xbox, go to the Microsoft Store on your console or online, search for "Wobbly Life," and download it. For PlayStation, visit the PlayStation Store, search for "Wobbly Life," and install the game on your console. For Nintendo Switch, open the Nintendo eShop, search for "Wobbly Life," and download it to your console.

Beginner's Guide

Start by exploring the town, interacting with objects, and completing your first simple tasks. Avoid falling off hills!
